At Fore Africa your safety is our 1st priority. Not only will you be as safe and secure as possible during your trip in SA - you will also experience the real South Africa and its splendid beauty. Best of all is the amazing scenery as well the wildlife in their natural surroundings. I, a born and bred South African, personally drive you through our scenic country and will try to answer all your questions about our culture, country, wildlife and anything else you might want to know.


Fore Africa specializes in trips from Gauteng to Sun City in the west all the way through to the east towards the amazing and well-known Kruger Park. On Route we will visit towns like Dullstroom, Sabie, Pilgrims Rest and Graskop. From Graskop we will follow the Panorama route with the Blyde River Canyon and all its spectacular viewpoints before entering the Kruger Park.

  • Drive & Guided tours for families and friends. Sit back and enjoy while I take care of all your needs.
