FORE AFRICA

PERSONAL AND PRIVATE GUIDED TOURS, I DRIVE GUIDED TOURS, INCENTIVE TOURS, GOLF & SAFARI TOURS AS WELL AS ADVENTURE TOURS

At Fore Africa your safety is our 1st priority. Not only will you be as safe and secure as possible during your trip in SA - you will also experience the real South Africa and its splendid beauty. Best of all is the amazing scenery as well as the wildlife in their natural surroundings. I, a born and bred South African, from Sabie will personally drive you through our scenic country and will try to answer all your questions about our culture, country, wildlife and anything else you might want to know. I'm a qualified guide for Mpumalanga, hold a Diploma in Journalism and I have 20 years experience in the hospitality industry. with Sun International.


Fore Africa specializes in trips from Gauteng to Sun City in the west, all the way through to the east towards the amazing and well-known Kruger Park. From Johannesburg and Pretoria we will visit towns like Dullstroom, Sabie, Pilgrims Rest and Graskop. From Graskop we will follow the Panorama route with the Blyde River Canyon and all its spectacular viewpoints before entering the Kruger Park. On route, I might just show you a few secret places only the locals knows about! For the more adventurous there is also cross border trip to the unspoilt Mozambique! My favorite qoute: " It is an It is real adventure getting there, but once you arrive at your destination on the coast - you will find an unspoilt paradise that only Africa can offer you "//
